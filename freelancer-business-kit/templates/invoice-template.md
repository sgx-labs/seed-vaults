## Usage Notes (Remove Before Sending)

- Always use a specific due date, not "Net 15"
- Include a direct payment link for fastest payment
- Send the invoice the same day work is delivered
- For retainer clients, send on the 1st of each month
- Track all invoices in your accounting software
- Follow up on Day 1 past due (see `research/invoicing/late-payment-procedures.md`)
