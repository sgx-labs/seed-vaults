# Freelance Services Agreement

**This agreement is between:**

**Freelancer:** [YOUR NAME / BUSINESS NAME]
[Address]
[Email]

**Client:** [CLIENT NAME / COMPANY NAME]
[Address]
[Email]

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

---

## 1. Services

Freelancer will provide the services described in the attached Scope of Work (Exhibit A). Any work not explicitly described in the Scope of Work is outside the scope of this agreement and may be subject to additional fees.

## 2. Payment

### 2.1 Compensation
Client will pay Freelancer the total fee of $[AMOUNT] according to the payment schedule outlined in Exhibit A.

### 2.2 Payment Terms
Invoices are due within [15] days of receipt. Payment may be made by [credit card, ACH transfer, wire transfer, check — specify accepted methods].

### 2.3 Deposit
A non-refundable deposit of [X]% ($[AMOUNT]) is due upon execution of this agreement. Work will not commence until the deposit is received.

### 2.4 Late Payments
Payments not received within [15] days of the due date will incur a late fee of 1.5% per month (18% annually) on the outstanding balance. Freelancer reserves the right to pause work on overdue accounts until the balance is paid in full.

## 3. Timeline

The project timeline is outlined in Exhibit A. Freelancer will use reasonable efforts to meet the agreed schedule. Delays caused by Client — including but not limited to late feedback, delayed access to materials or systems, or changed requirements — will extend the timeline by a corresponding period.

## 4. Revisions and Changes

### 4.1 Included Revisions
This agreement includes [2] rounds of revisions per deliverable as defined in Exhibit A. A revision round consists of one consolidated set of changes submitted in writing.

### 4.2 Additional Revisions
Revisions beyond the included rounds will be billed at $[HOURLY RATE]/hour.

### 4.3 Scope Changes
Changes to the project scope require a written change order describing the additional work, associated cost, and timeline impact. No out-of-scope work will begin until the change order is signed by both parties.

## 5. Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Ownership During Project
All work product created under this agreement remains the property of Freelancer until final payment is received in full. Client receives a limited, non-transferable license to review and evaluate deliverables during the project.

### 5.2 Assignment on Payment
Upon receipt of final payment in full, Freelancer hereby assigns to Client all rights, title, and interest in the deliverables described in Exhibit A, including all applicable copyrights.

### 5.3 Portfolio Rights
Freelancer retains the right to display and reference the work in their portfolio, website, and marketing materials.

### 5.4 Pre-Existing Materials
Tools, frameworks, libraries, or methodologies created by Freelancer prior to or independent of this engagement remain the property of Freelancer. Client receives a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use any such materials incorporated into the deliverables.

## 6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary or sensitive information shared during this engagement. This obligation survives termination of this agreement for a period of [2] years. Confidentiality does not apply to information that is publicly available or independently developed.

## 7. Termination

### 7.1 By Either Party
Either party may terminate this agreement with [14] days written notice.

### 7.2 Payment on Termination
Upon termination, Client will pay for all work completed to the date of termination, plus any expenses incurred.

### 7.3 Kill Fee
If Client terminates without cause before completion, a kill fee of [25]% of the remaining unpaid project balance applies.

### 7.4 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately for material breach, including but not limited to non-payment, if the breach is not cured within [7] days of written notice.

## 8. Limitation of Liability

Freelancer's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client. In no event shall Freelancer be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages, including lost profits.

## 9. Independent Contractor

Freelancer is an independent contractor, not an employee of Client. Freelancer is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and benefits. Nothing in this agreement creates an employer-employee, partnership, or joint venture relationship.

## 10. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this agreement will first be addressed through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved within [30] days, disputes will be submitted to binding arbitration in [YOUR CITY/STATE] under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

## 11. General

- This agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.
- Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
- This agreement is governed by the laws of [YOUR STATE].
- If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in effect.
