## How to get started

1. Pick a topic you know well
2. Copy the [`_seed-template/`](./_seed-template/) directory
3. Start with 10-15 notes — you can always add more
4. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for quality guidelines
5. Submit a PR

These are just starting points. The best seeds come from your own expertise. If there's something you find yourself explaining to colleagues over and over, that's a seed waiting to happen.
