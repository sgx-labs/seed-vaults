# Bootstrap — New Session Quick Start

## Water the Seed

First time? Your agent will handle everything. Just run:

```bash
same init && same reindex
```

Then start a conversation. Your agent reads this file automatically and begins the Research Cascade -- a multi-phase process that scans your team context, understands your management challenges, asks smart questions, and fills your vault with personalized frameworks. The more you interact, the smarter it gets.

**What happens next (automatic):**
1. Agent silently scans your project -- team docs, processes, org structure, existing frameworks
2. Agent presents an insight report showing what it learned about your team
3. Agent asks 3-5 targeted questions about your management challenges and pain points
4. Agent proposes CLAUDE.md additions (archives originals safely)
5. Agent recommends research topics specific to YOUR team's situation
6. You pick which topics matter -- agents research in parallel, save results to your vault
7. Each research result suggests new topics -- the vault grows exponentially

After the first session, your vault has generic management knowledge PLUS team-specific intelligence that no one else has.

## Returning Sessions

Already set up? Here is what your agent does automatically on every session:

- **Loads context** -- SAME surfaces your pinned notes, recent handoffs, and decisions
- **Remembers where you left off** -- session handoffs capture what was done and what is pending
- **Searches before generating** -- your vault has 60+ curated notes, growing with every session
- **Cross-references** -- finds related frameworks you might not know about
- **Compounds knowledge** -- every useful answer gets saved for next time

## Key Questions This Seed Answers

1. How do I run 1-on-1s that my reports actually look forward to?
2. How do I write performance reviews that drive real growth?
3. How do I build a fair, consistent hiring process?
4. How do I handle underperformance without destroying someone's confidence?
5. What should I delegate and what should I keep close?
6. How do I detect team health problems before they become crises?
7. When should I make the technical call vs let the team decide?
8. How do I run postmortems that actually prevent recurrence?
9. Which retrospective format works best for my team right now?
10. How do I set up a new engineer for success from day one?
