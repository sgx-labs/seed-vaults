# Common Meeting Cadences

## By Team Size

### Small Team (3-5 engineers)

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Notes |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------|
| Standup | Daily | 10 min | Can be async in Slack |
| 1-on-1s | Weekly | 30 min | |
| Sprint planning | Biweekly | 30 min | Light -- scope is obvious |
| Retro | Biweekly | 30 min | |
| **Total per person** | | **~3 hrs/week** | |

### Medium Team (6-10 engineers)

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Notes |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------|
| Standup | Daily | 15 min | Keep strict -- no discussions |
| 1-on-1s | Weekly | 30 min | |
| Sprint planning | Biweekly | 60 min | |
| Retro | Biweekly | 45 min | |
| Team sync | Weekly | 30 min | Cross-cutting topics, demos |
| Tech deep-dive | Biweekly | 60 min | Design reviews, architecture |
| **Total per person** | | **~5 hrs/week** | |

### Large Team (10+ engineers, multiple squads)

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Notes |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------|
| Squad standup | Daily | 15 min | Per squad |
| 1-on-1s | Weekly | 30 min | |
| Squad planning | Biweekly | 45 min | Per squad |
| Squad retro | Biweekly | 45 min | Per squad |
| All-hands team sync | Weekly | 30 min | Cross-squad updates |
| Tech council | Biweekly | 60 min | Leads from each squad |
| **Total per person** | | **~5-6 hrs/week** | |

## Manager Cadences

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | With |
|---------|-----------|----------|------|
| 1-on-1s with reports | Weekly | 30 min each | Direct reports |
| 1-on-1 with your manager | Weekly | 30 min | Your manager |
| EM peer sync | Weekly | 30 min | Peer managers |
| Skip-levels | Monthly | 30 min | Reports' reports |
| Staff meeting | Weekly | 60 min | Leadership team |

## Warning Signs

- **> 6 hours of meetings per week per IC**: Too much. Cut something.
- **No meeting-free days**: Engineers need blocks of uninterrupted time.
- **Recurring meetings with no agenda**: Kill them or fix them.
- **Standup taking > 15 minutes**: You're having discussions, not standups.
