# LinkedIn — Job Search Tool

## What It Is
The primary professional networking platform. For job seekers, it serves as a searchable resume, networking tool, job board, and research platform.

## Key Features for Job Seekers
- **Profile** — your digital resume that recruiters search by keyword
- **Job Search** — filter by role, location, company, remote, experience level
- **Easy Apply** — one-click applications (lower quality, higher volume)
- **Open to Work** — signals to recruiters you are looking (can be visible to all or recruiters only)
- **Networking** — connect with employees at target companies
- **Company Pages** — research culture, recent posts, employee count trends

## Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Professional headshot (profiles with photos get 14x more views)
- [ ] Keyword-optimized headline (not just your job title)
- [ ] Detailed About section in first person
- [ ] Experience with accomplishment-driven bullets
- [ ] 50 skills added and top 3 pinned
- [ ] Custom URL (linkedin.com/in/yourname)
- [ ] Open to Work enabled (recruiter-only visibility)
- [ ] Activity: regular comments and shares in your industry
