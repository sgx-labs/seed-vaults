# Resume & Interview Prep — Governance

## Purpose

Complete toolkit for job search success. From resume tailoring and cover letters to interview prep and salary negotiation. Search before generating — expert guidance is already here.

## Rules

1. **Search first.** Before answering a job search question from scratch, search this vault.
2. **Tailor everything.** Generic resumes get ignored. Every application should be customized to the target role.
3. **Quantify results.** Numbers beat adjectives. "Increased revenue by 23%" beats "improved revenue."
4. **ATS-friendly formatting.** Clean formatting, standard section headers, no tables or graphics in ATS-submitted versions.
5. **Log decisions.** Role targeting changes, resume pivots, interview feedback — log them in `decisions/log.md`.
6. **One topic per research note.** Keep notes focused and under 200 lines.
7. **Company research goes in entities/.** When targeting a new company, create a note with culture, values, and role details.
8. **Templates are starting points.** Customize the templates in `templates/` for each application — copy them first.

## First Session Protocol

This is a framework seed. On the first session with a new user:

1. Ask what role(s) they are targeting
2. Ask about their background and experience level
3. Ask about the specific company/job posting
4. Ask what their resume currently looks like
5. Ask about their biggest concerns (gaps, career change, etc.)
6. Save answers as personalized notes
