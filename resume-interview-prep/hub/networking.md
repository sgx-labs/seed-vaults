# Networking & Referrals

## Overview

Referred candidates are hired at 3-5x the rate of cold applicants. This hub covers optimizing your LinkedIn profile for job search, reaching out to connections for warm introductions, conducting informational interviews, writing outreach messages that get responses, building relationships before you need them, and leveraging alumni and professional networks.

## Key Principles

1. **Referrals beat cold applications.** A referred resume goes to the top of the pile.
2. **Give before you ask.** Build relationships with value before requesting favors.
3. **Be specific in your ask.** "Can you introduce me to the hiring manager for the Senior Analyst role?" beats "Let me know if you hear of anything."
4. **Follow up and close the loop.** Always update people who helped you on the outcome.
