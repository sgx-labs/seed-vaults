# Behavioral Interview Question Bank

## The Core Idea

Behavioral questions follow the pattern "Tell me about a time when..." They assume past behavior predicts future performance. For each category below, you should have at least one prepared STAR story.

## Leadership & Influence

- Tell me about a time you led a project or team.
- Describe a situation where you had to influence someone without direct authority.
- Tell me about a time you mentored or developed someone.
- Give an example of a time you had to make a difficult decision with incomplete information.

## Conflict & Challenges

- Tell me about a time you had a disagreement with a coworker. How did you handle it?
- Describe a situation where you received critical feedback. What did you do with it?
- Tell me about a time you had to push back on a stakeholder request.
- Give an example of navigating a politically sensitive situation at work.

## Problem-Solving & Initiative

- Tell me about a complex problem you solved.
- Describe a time you identified an issue before it became a problem.
- Tell me about a time you improved a process or system.
- Give an example of when you had to learn something quickly to complete a task.

## Teamwork & Collaboration

- Tell me about a time you worked with a difficult team member.
- Describe a successful cross-functional project you were part of.
- Tell me about a time you had to compromise to reach a team goal.

## Failure & Growth

- Tell me about a time you failed. What did you learn?
- Describe a mistake you made at work. How did you handle it?
- Tell me about a goal you did not achieve. What happened?

## Time Management & Pressure

- Tell me about a time you had to manage competing priorities.
- Describe a situation where you delivered under a tight deadline.
- Give an example of a time you had to say no to a request.

## Preparation Tip

For each category, write out a full STAR story. Practice it until you can deliver it naturally in 60-90 seconds. You do not need a unique story for every question — one strong story often covers multiple categories with different emphasis.
