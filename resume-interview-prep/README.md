# Resume & Interview Prep — SAME Seed Vault

A complete toolkit for job search success built on the [SAME](https://github.com/sgx-labs/statelessagent) framework.

## Quick Start

```bash
same init
same reindex
same search "how do I tailor my resume"
```

## What's Inside

- **Resume writing** — tailoring, bullet points, ATS optimization, formatting
- **Interview prep** — STAR method, behavioral questions, company research
- **Salary negotiation** — tactics, total comp evaluation, handling salary questions
- **Networking** — LinkedIn optimization, referrals, informational interviews
- **Templates** — resume checklist, STAR stories, interview prep sheet, thank-you emails

See `bootstrap.md` for full orientation.

## Personalizing This Seed

This is a **framework seed** — it provides the structure and expert knowledge, and you fill in your specific details. On your first session:

1. Tell the agent what role you are targeting
2. Share the job posting
3. Let the agent interview you about your background
4. Your personalized notes will be saved into the vault
