# STAR Story Preparation Template

Copy this template for each story. Prepare 8-10 stories covering different behavioral categories.

---

## Story Title: [Descriptive Name]

**Category:** [Leadership / Conflict / Failure / Teamwork / Initiative / Problem-solving / Time pressure / Influencing]

**Best for answering:** [List 2-3 behavioral questions this story answers well]

### Situation (2-3 sentences)
[Set the scene. When, where, what was the context?]

### Task (1-2 sentences)
[What was YOUR specific responsibility or challenge?]

### Action (4-6 sentences)
[What did YOU do? Be specific. Use "I" not "we." Include your reasoning.]

### Result (2-3 sentences)
[What was the outcome? Quantify. What did you learn?]

### Key Talking Points
- [The most impressive part of this story]
- [The skill it demonstrates]
- [The learning or growth it shows]

### Timing Check
Target: 60-90 seconds. Practice with a timer.
